Tzatziki

Need an unexciting dip for your extracurricular bible study tonight?

Ingredients

  • 1,5 cucumber
  • 0,5 tbs salt
  • 2 garlic wedges
  • 3 dl greek yogurt
  • 0,5 tbs freshly ground pepper

Cook it:

  1. Roughly grate the cucumber and sprinkle with salt. Leave for 5-10 mins, then squeeze the water out of it
  2. Mix with finely minced garlic and yogurt, season to taste with salt and pepper

There you go! A dip that will offend noone whilst also satisfying noone. Nobody wins!
