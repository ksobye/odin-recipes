Rhubarb soup

How to make a delicious rhubarb soup using only rhubarb and soup

Ingredients

  • 250 g rhubarb
  • 1 l water (for boiling)
  • 200 g sugar
  • 1,5 tbs potato flour
  • 1 dl water (for potato flour mix)

Cook it:

  1. Wash and peel rhubarb
  2. Boil the rhubarb in a pot with water and sugar until they come apart, 5-10 mins
  3. Stir the potato flour into 1 dl of water unti evenly mixed, slowly pour into pot while stirring. Bring to a boil and soup's done!
  4. For cold soup - your fridge makes things cold

Serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream for extra yums.
